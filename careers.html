CAREERS

Vicara offers an open work culture with a flat structure that empowers individuals to contribute their best and grow to their potential. Our projects are diverse and our customers demanding. We believe strongly that everybody is served best when each gets to do what they like to do best. We challenge our staff to maintain a healthy work-life balance and to grow their interests outside of work

Learning is a culture and we never stop doing so. This is as much a fact of life as it is an expectation we have to ourselves. People who join Vicara or who are interested in joining us should be highly motivated individuals with a background in DWH & Business Intelligence or Big Data or one of the following areas:

  • PROJECT/PROGRAM MANAGEMENT
  • BUSINESS ANALYSIS/ REQUIREMENTS ENGINEERING
  • SOFTWARE ENGINEERING
  • TESTING / QA
