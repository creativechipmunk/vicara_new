MANGED CAPACITY IT CONSULTING

Project 1

  • Client: Large Swiss-based multi-national bank

  • Engagement area: Group Reporting (IT)

  • Objective: Key Project Staff (PM, BA, DEV, Test plus Functional Lead staff)

  • Speciality: Provided key project resources to support large strategic development of a group-wide Data Warehouse and Reporting solution for Risk and Finance Reporting. Net new development of solution platform (15+ data warehouse and reporting solutions) for multiple divisions plus group. Integration of many divisional source systems. Construct of highly complex client data confidentiality regulations.

  • Outcome: Successful delivery of solutions. Highly appreciated project staff moving across sub-projects to support and trouble shoot problem areas. Some projects are still ongoing

Project 2

  • Client: Large Swiss-based multi-national bank

  • Engagement area: Corporate Security IT

  • Objective: Deliver Advanced Analytics solution for Identity and Access Management

  • Speciality: On-shore/off-shore mix consisting of project manager, business analysts, data modeler, solution architect, engineers, testing team data modeling, development and unit testing done from Indian Development Center. Development of an Advanced Analytics solution for Identity and Access Management enabling users in IT Security and Business Risk to analyze and identify weaknesses in its overall security and access management framework for the entire bank

  • Outcome: In-time, in-budget delivery of the software

Managed services

  • Client: Large Swiss-based multi-national bank

  • Engagement area: Corporate and Institutional Clients business division

  • Objective: Outsource and partially off-shore 10+ Data Warehouse and Customer Relationship software components to Vicara. Multi-year KTLO (Run the Bank) service including Level 3 Support.

  • Speciality: On-shore/off-shore mix, second and third level support, IT product management of the software components. Primary delivery resources and client side contacts are based in Switzerland. Engineering, testing and support staff is based in India.

  • Outcome: Project is ongoing. Transition has been successfully completed and currently in a steady state where by the application area is fully managed by Vicara, the delivery team is embedded in the client side structures and processes.

BUSINESS AND FINANCIAL ANALYSIS

  • Objective: Provide Business Analysts to support Application Migration Testing

  • Speciality: What was an initial request to support Application Testing and Migration Support developed into a key staff augmentation. Responsibility to optimise month-end processes and manage data driven end-user applications and processes (high billion dollar reports).

TESTING & QUALITY ASSURANCE

  • Client: Large Swiss-based multi-national bank

  • Engagement area: Group Analytical Services

  • Objective: Development and Integration of Synthetic Test Data

  • Speciality: Development of multi-use test data across entire segments of a Business System Data Warehouse.Test data was developed off-shore in and off-line, with no direct access to the end-user systems.

  • Outcome: Successful implementation of the test data. Development was successfully completed with minimal interaction or disruption of on-site development teams and application owners. Test data is implemented for Swiss as well as international development teams. Data purpose is for business as well as User Acceptance Testing (UAT).
